Yo, I'm the one behind Overzealous Premed.

I started this because the premed process felt way harder than it needed to be. Not because the material is impossible but because nobody tells you what actually matters vs what's just noise. I wasted too much time stressing about stuff that didn't move the needle and I don't want you making the same mistakes I did.

So I started making videos. Honest ones. About what it's really like. The wins, the Ls, the late nights, the breakthroughs. Then people started asking for more direct help so I opened up mentorship.

If you're just starting to think about medicine or deep in application season, I've probably made a video or worked with someone in your exact spot. Lets figure it out.
