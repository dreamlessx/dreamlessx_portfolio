methods / pipeline

End-to-end computational pipeline integrating AI structure prediction, physics-based relaxation, sequence design, and quality validation.

01

structure prediction

Ran AlphaFold 2.3.2 and Boltz-1 v0.4.1 across the full BM5.5 protein-protein docking benchmark (257 complexes). Generated initial structure predictions for all bound and unbound conformations, providing the raw material for downstream benchmarking.

AlphaFold 2.3.2 Boltz-1 v0.4.1 BM5.5
02

AMBER relaxation

Applied AMBER force field relaxation on all AlphaFold and Boltz-1 predicted models. This physics-based energy minimization step resolves steric clashes and refines local geometry from the AI-predicted structures.

AMBER OpenMM
03

Rosetta relaxation

Ran 6 Rosetta 3.15 relaxation protocols with 5 replicates each across all 257 complexes. This systematic approach tests multiple energy landscapes and sampling strategies to find the most physically realistic conformations.

Rosetta 3.15 FastRelax 6 protocols x 5 replicates
04

sequence design

Built an end-to-end computational pipeline integrating AlphaFold, Rosetta, and ProteinMPNN for protein design. ProteinMPNN generates optimized amino acid sequences conditioned on the relaxed backbone structures, targeting improved stability for alphavirus vaccine candidates.

ProteinMPNN AlphaFold Rosetta
05

quality validation

Validated all generated structures using MolProbity quality metrics (clashscore, Ramachandran outliers, rotamer analysis) and PoseBusters geometry validation (bond lengths, angles, steric clashes). This ensures structural integrity before any downstream biological conclusions.

MolProbity PoseBusters Python

results

Key outcomes from the BM5.5 benchmarking effort and protein design pipeline.

full BM5.5 benchmark

Built the complete protein-protein complex relaxation benchmark across all 257 complexes in the Protein-Protein Docking Benchmark 5.5. This is the first systematic comparison of AI-predicted vs. experimentally-derived structures at this scale with multi-protocol Rosetta relaxation.

257 complexes / 6,800+ structures

multi-protocol relaxation

Systematic evaluation of 6 distinct Rosetta relaxation protocols, each with 5 independent replicates, generating a comprehensive landscape of relaxed conformations. Enables robust statistical comparison of protocol performance across diverse complex types.

6 protocols x 5 replicates x 257 complexes

structure quality metrics

MolProbity validation across all structures: clashscore analysis, Ramachandran favored/outlier percentages, rotamer quality, and Cbeta deviations. PoseBusters geometry checks confirm physical plausibility of AI-generated models post-relaxation.

molprobity + posebusters on all models

protein design pipeline

End-to-end integration of structure prediction, relaxation, and inverse folding for alphavirus vaccine stabilization. The pipeline takes a target complex, predicts structure, relaxes, designs sequences, and validates, all automated.

alphafold + rosetta + proteinmpnn
